There are several categories of diets these days that involve not eating certain foods, there are some examples below. If you hover your mouse here the list will disappear.

  1. Belief-based diets, such as kosher or buddhist
  2. Vegetarian diets, such as vegan or pescetarian
  3. Weight-control diets
  4. Crash/fad diets, ususally these have severe health risks and are not recommended
  5. Detox diets, such as a juice cleanse
  6. Diets followed for medical reasons, such as allergies
  7. Miscellaneous diets, usually excluding a specific food type or food not included in the other options listed above
